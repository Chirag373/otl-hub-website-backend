PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: January 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SKF Multi Solutions, LLC, operating the OTLHub platform (“OTLHub,” “we,” “us,” or “our”), collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal information when you access or use our website, applications, and services (collectively, the “Platform”).

By using the Platform, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information you provide directly and information collected automatically through your use of the Platform.

1.1 Information You Provide

Depending on your role (Homeowner, Buyer/Investor, Realtor, or Partner), we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Account login credentials
  • Property-related information you choose to share
  • Professional license information (for Realtors or licensed professionals)
  • Business details (for Partners)
  • Messages sent through the Platform
  • Subscription or billing-related information (processed via third-party providers)

⚠️ OTLHub does not intentionally collect sensitive personal data such as Social Security numbers, bank account numbers, or government-issued IDs.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you access the Platform, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Device type, browser type, and operating system
  • Usage data (pages viewed, time spent, interactions)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

This information helps us improve platform performance and user experience.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and operate the Platform
  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Enable communication between users
  • Display listings, profiles, and partner promotions
  • Process subscriptions and platform access
  • Improve functionality, security, and usability
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, or unauthorized use

3. HOW INFORMATION IS SHARED

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only as follows:

3.1 With Other Users

Information you voluntarily post (such as listings, profiles, or messages) may be visible to other users according to your account settings.

3.2 With Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third-party vendors who assist with:

  • Hosting
  • Payment processing
  • Email communications
  • Analytics and security

These providers are contractually required to protect your data.

3.3 Legal and Compliance

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect rights, safety, or property.

6. COOKIES AND TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES

7. DATA RETENTION
